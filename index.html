1000 Gags:The Gag Chart

This Gag Chart/Word Generator is based on the one found in the book "1000 Gags a Year" which was published in 1951 By Cartoon Consultants and written by legendary cartoonists Jack Markow,Harry Lampert, and Dan Koener. I first came upon the book thanks to the high rez scans on Mike Lynch's Cartooning Blog and thought I could replicate that via a "web app" and here we are(with some help from a good buddy Ramon!) I've added more "Ideas" to the "Gag Chart" and if you have any suggestions, please send them along to my email
Finally, if you found this useful, I wouldn't mind a little donation, no amount is too little. Thanks for stopping by and hope this helps!
